Participants will first gain a solid understanding of WireGuard fundamentals and learn how to configure it within RouterOS. The lesson also explains the key technical differences between EoIP and WireGuard, focusing on their protocol design and configuration approaches.

To deepen this understanding, participants will then perform a hands-on exercise, configuring and combining WireGuard and EoIP in a practical scenario. They will implement Layer 2 tunnels with EoIP over WireGuard, a setup used for remote debugging and specialized bridging tasks.
